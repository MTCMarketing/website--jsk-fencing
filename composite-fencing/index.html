Composite Fencing FAQs

What is composite fencing made of?

Composite fencing is a premium blend of recycled wood fibers and high-density plastic. This combination provides the authentic look of natural wood with the superior durability of synthetic materials.

How long does composite fencing last?

Composite fences are incredibly durable and typically last 20-30 years with minimal maintenance. They are resistant to rot, warping, and insect damage.

Does composite fencing fade over time?

While all outdoor materials experience some UV exposure, high-quality composite fencing is engineered with UV stabilizers to maintain its rich color and finish for decades.
